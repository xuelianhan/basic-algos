Job Summary:
We are looking for a talented Cloud Administrator/Architect to design, implement, manage, and optimize
our cloud-based systems. The successful candidate will be responsible for overseeing the architecture,
deployment, and maintenance of our cloud infrastructure, ensuring high availability, scalability, and
security. This role requires a deep understanding of cloud technologies, strong problem-solving skills,
and the ability to collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams.
Key Responsibilities:
- Design and deploy cloud infrastructure solutions that meet the company's
 operational and security requirements.
- Manage and maintain cloud resources, including virtual machines, storage,
 networks, and databases, across various cloud platforms (e.g., mainly Azure).
- Implement and optimize cloud-based applications, ensuring seamless integration
 and performance.
- Monitor system performance, troubleshoot issues, and implement necessary
 fixes to ensure high availability and reliability.
- Collaborate with development and IT teams to provide guidance on cloud
 best practices, architecture, and solutions.
- Ensure data security and compliance by implementing appropriate security
 measures and protocols.
- Document system configurations, procedures, and troubleshooting steps.
Qualifications:
- Proven experience as a Cloud Administrator/Architect or similar role.
- Proficiency in Azure cloud platform
- Experience with containerization technologies (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes) is a plus.
- Excellent scripting and automation skills (e.g., PowerShell, Bash, Python).
- Familiarity with DevOps practices and tools (e.g., CI/CD pipelines).
- Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
- Relevant cloud certifications (e.g., AWS Certified Solutions Architect,
 Azure Solutions Architect) are a plus
